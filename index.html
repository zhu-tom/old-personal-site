About Me Who am I?

My name is Tom Zhu and I'm currently a second-year Computer Science Student at Carleton University. I was born and raised in Ottawa, and I've been playing sports for as long as I can remember. I started programming in the 11th grade and haven't looked back since. I like building my own full-stack projects and learning new technology and frameworks. My main focuses have been with web and mobile development. I'm a huge fan of basketball and volleyball, and I love playing the guitar and watching movies. Check out my work down below!
